Sponsorship Information

Business are invited to sponsor the South Island Straight Liners Racing Association.

SISLRA is working to reduce or stop the number of people involved in crashes as a result of high speed street racing. Our mission is "Drag racers against street racing". Entirely volunteer run, our non-profit society has teamed up with the Saanich Police Department to host a Student Trophy Class in addition to our existing Pro, Street, and Motorcycle Classes, encouraging young and adult drivers to race at Western Speedway in a safe and controlled enviroment.

Businesses are welcome to sponsor as many half or full races as they like. Sponsorship on a smaller scale can also be made by way of a smaller monetary donation or by donating products or services that can be used as prizes.

Sponsorship has its rewards;

  • A $600.00 monetary sponsorship gives your business exclusive advertising for one full race.
  • A $300.00 monetary sponsorship gives your business advertising for half of one race.

Advertising includes;

  • Entitlement to a 4' by 8' area on the burnout wall in which your business has the option to provide a sign for further advertising for the season. This sign can be seen by all drag racing participants and spectators who attend all events hosted at Western Speedway.
  • Your business logo will be printed on the club website for the season.
  • Your business name will appear on all time slips on your sponsored race day.
  • Your business name and advertisement will be announced on race day.

We welcome you to be part of the solution; race at the track, not on the streets.
