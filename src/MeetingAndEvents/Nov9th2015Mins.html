November 9th 2015 Meeting Minutes


South Island Straight Liners Racing Association
November 9, 2015 Meeting Minutes
Commonwealth Pool, Pacific Sport Entrance
4636 Elk Lake Drive, Victoria, B.C.

In Attendance:
Randy Strugnell, Lisa Miller, Blake Miller, Clint Neargarth, Dave van Humbeck, Bill Jamieson Jr., Jim Hannah, Gayle Hebert, Connor Barnes, Kole Barnes, Tracy Barnes, Karolee Garlock, Kevin Garlock.
 
Chair, Co-President: Clint Neargarth
Co-President: Randy Strugnell
Treasurer: Bill Jamieson Jr.
Secretary: Lisa Miller
Race Director: Christy McCrae (absent)
 
 
Called to Order: 7:00 pm (1st Clint Neargarth, 2nd Lisa Miller)
 
December 13, 2014 meeting minutes accepted (1st Jim Hannah, 2nd Dave van Humbeck)
 
**OLD BUSINESS:
 
1. Win Lights Issue:
Win lights are working. Thank you to Wade Barnes and others for the fix.
 
2. Significant Decline in "Street" and "Bike" Class Racers:
The decline in "Street" and "Bike" class vehicles at all races this year was discussed.
 
3. Sponsorship 2015:
Members thanked our sponsors this year for their support. Sponsorship was low this year. Two lanes were sponsored and two races were sponsored. Members discussed that hopefully next year sponsorship will increase.
 
**NEW BUSINESS:
 
1. Silent Auction, Awards Night and Annual General Meeting 2015:
Members were asked for silent auction donations for the December 12, 2015 Silent Auction. Please contact Bill Jamieson, Jr. if you have any items to donate or any questions. The event will be at the Six Mile Pub this year at 494 Old Island Highway at 7:00 pm. The event is an important fundraiser for the club as well as the awards night and Annual General Meeting. All ages (including those under 19) are welcome to this event. Members are encouraged to bring family, friends, and sponsors.
 
2. Pro Class and Street Class cut-off.
Blake Miller brought up the fact that in the early years of the clubs history, the time cut-off between Pro Class and Street Class was 5.8 seconds and was changed to 6.0 seconds due to the lack of Pro Class cars; and that our recent problem is the lack of Street Class cars. Blake recommended that we go back to the 5.8 seconds time cut-off to attempt to even the class numbers. – This item is AGM motion #1 below.
 
3. Track compound will be needed for next season.
Randy noted that the club will need to purchase track compound (i.e., VHT) for next season and that this is a high cost item. Members discussed that the silent auction is an important fundraiser for such costs.
 
4. Volunteer near end of the track.
Tracy Barnes noted that a volunteer near the end of the track during racing would make racing run smoother. This volunteer would be able to help with end of track situations: for example; light resets, wildlife monitoring, aiding to broken down vehicles, and others. Kevin and Karolee Garlock noted that they pit at the end of the track and offered that when Kevin is not in line to race or racing, they will volunteer. Members discussed to advise all racers as well to watch for these instances when Kevin and Karolee are not available.
 
5. Fire Lane Issues.
Tracy Barnes noted that people have been abusing the fire lane access to the track.
Members are reminded that they are not to use the fire lane access to the track. This area is the straight drive in to the track along the fence or treed area parallel to the RV park. Racers are to line up behind the other racers for entry. The fire lane access will be blocked with cones this year due to this past year where racers have been driving in ahead of those who have been correctly waiting in line. Members are reminded that only volunteers approved by executive can enter prior to those in the line due to time needed to complete their duties (i.e., techs, track preparation and set up).
 
6. Executive Nominations for 2016 to date.
Members nominated for positions 2016 are as follows:
President: Clint Neargarth (Nominated by: Randy Strugnell)
Vice President: Tracy Barnes (Nominated by: Lisa Miller)
Treasurer: Bill Jamieson, Jr. (Nominated by: Blake Miller)
Secretary: Lisa Miller (Nominated by Clint Neargarth)
Race Director(s): Jim Hannah and Kole Barnes (As Co-Race Directors - Nominated by Clint Neargarth and Tracy Barnes).
 
**AGM MOTION for vote
AGM MOTION 1: by Blake Miller, 2nded by Dave van Humbeck.
 
Change the time cutoff between Pro Class and Street Class to 5.8 seconds (this will make all cars 5.8 seconds and slower Street Class participants and 5.79 seconds and faster Pro Class participants).
 
EXECUTIVE NOMINATIONS 2016 AND VOTING:
POSITION
 
President: Clint Neargarth
Vice President: Tracy Barnes
Treasurer: Bill Jamieson, Jr.
Co Race Director(s): Jim Hannah and Kole Barnes
Secretary: Lisa Miller
 
Motion to adjourn by Randy Strugnell (1st Lisa Miller; and 2nd Bill Jamieson, Jr.) at 8:02 pm.
 
**NEXT MEETING** 2015
 
Annual General Meeting, Silent Auction and Awards Night
All ages welcome (inclusive of 19 and under).
 
Date: December 12, 2015
Time: 7:00 pm
Location: Six Mile Pub, 494 Old Island Highway.
