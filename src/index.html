Memberships Have Their Rewards

By having a membership, both racers and non-racers have access to discounts on race days. Benefits include; a discount on car and driver fees for racers, a discount on pit entry fees, voting rights at meetings, receive club emails of events & updates, participation in the points class standings for racers, discounts at Lordco, etc...

Help support our club and ensure we are racing for many years to come.

Individual Memberships are $30.00 and family Memberships are $60.00 (Family Memberships includes two adults and two children under 16) and run from April 1st to May 31st annually.

Memberships can be purchased at monthly meetings, on race day, or by contacting the Membership Director - Bill Jamieson at 250-888-0053 or though email at mrpontiac55@gmail.com
